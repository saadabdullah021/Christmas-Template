About Us

At the Christmasly Shop our mission is to offer "Unique Christmas Decor You Won't Find in a Big-Box Store". We're a small local business located in Smith Field Square Dublin 7, Ireland. The Christmasly Shop started as an online Christmas shop in 2012, handpicking each item with the magic of Christmas in mind. Our site offers 1,000's of Christmas decorations, 85% of those items are new for that year. We share our love of Christmas all around the world, now shipping to more than 30 countries. We have a dedicated shipping team that works day and night during peak season to make sure you order goes out quickly and efficiently with most orders shipping within 24 hours. Here, at The Christmasly Shop, we strive every single day for excellence, to help our customers as best we can, and to wish you a very merry Christmas Season! We hope to help you make this Christmas and every Christmas following extremely special!
